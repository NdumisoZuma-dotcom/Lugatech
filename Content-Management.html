Technical SEO(Including Google Ads and Facebook ads)

  • Lugatech
  • Jun 13, 2022

You might be wondering, what is technical SEO? and why does my website need SEO?

SEO is the abbreviation for Search Engine Optimization. This is the process of improving the quality and the amount of people finding your website from searching for poducts or services related to your business on search engines such as google search, Bing and other platforms. The more attention your website can attract the more chances of you getting brand new customers.

You can choose between having standard SEO which focuses on keyword strategies, image optimization, link structure, meta tags, site speed and appearing on google maps or advanced technical SEO which is all of the mentioned strategies including Google ads and Facebook ads.

At Lugatech we pride ourselves in delivering quality SEO Optimization to our customer's websites at reasonable prices. In the beginning of development or after we have deployed your website you can request standard technical SEO optimization for a low price of R600, once off or advanced SEO which will vary depending on the google ads and facebook ads you are willing to pay for.
